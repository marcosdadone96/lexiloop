Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. Contact: contact@lexicoil.com

LexiCoil helps you practise English and German official-style exams. This policy explains what data we collect and why.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data - name, email address, authentication identifiers (via Supabase when enabled).
  • Learning data - flashcards, exam history, saved exams, answers you submit (synced to our servers when you are signed in).
  • Usage and quota - exam generation counts, plan (guest / free / Pro), payment status via Stripe.
  • Technical data - IP address (for guest quota and rate limiting), browser type, cookies/local storage on your device.
  • AI processing - exam text and your speaking/writing answers may be sent to Anthropic's API to generate or evaluate content. We do not sell this data.

2. How we use your data

  • Provide and improve the LexiCoil service.
  • Sync your vocabulary and exam history across devices.
  • Enforce usage quotas and process payments.
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset).

3. Third parties

We use Supabase (auth/database), Stripe (payments), Netlify (hosting), and Anthropic (AI). Each has its own privacy policy.

4. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data by emailing contact@lexicoil.com.

5. Cookies and local storage

We store authentication tokens and preferences (theme, language) in your browser. No advertising trackers.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
